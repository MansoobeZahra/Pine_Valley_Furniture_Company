New Customer Registration

Go to the Registration page. Fill in all fields (Customer ID, name, address, city, state, postal code) and click Register.

Searching Products

Go to Search Products. Enter a Product ID, name, or select a Product Line. Click Search to see matching results. Click Order to place an order for that product.

Placing an Order

Go to Product Selection & Order Placement. Enter your Customer ID, Order ID, date, and up to three product lines with quantities. Click Place Order.

Updating Product Catalog

Go to Product Catalog Update. Add a new product using the form, or click Edit or Delete next to an existing product.

Making a Payment

Go to the Payment page. Enter your Order ID and Customer ID, choose a payment method, fill in card details if applicable, and click Make Payment.
